ABOUT THE MOVEMENT

This isn’t just a brand — it’s a rebellion. Pleading Sanity is where survivors, misfits, warriors, and truth-tellers rise. It’s for the ones called “mad” who turned their pain into art. We stand for mental strength, street wisdom, and unapologetic hope.

Founded by Shane Cooper (@mentally.inshane), this project started as a whisper in a padded room — and turned into a battle cry for the world. The clothing, the platform, the stories — all exist for one reason: to prove that madness is not a weakness. It’s a code. And we’re decoding it together.
